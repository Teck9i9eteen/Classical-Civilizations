The Roman Republic

Human and Physical Geography

During the Pax Romana (the peace which existed between nationalities within the Roman Empire), the rich and the wealthy (upper class) built huge, lavishly decorated houses as the peasents (lower class) tended to their every need. The average person (middle class) worked hard and well and lived in a very modest and average home. The romans had a diet revolving around grapes, grain, and olives. The rich and the wealthy were able to eat whatever they wanted, from jellyfish to peacock to ostrich and fungus. The more unusual the food seemed, the more they would want to eat it. They would also purge themselves to continue to eat. There was a myth about a place called the Vomitorium. It is said that the Vomitorium was a room where the rich would vomit their lavish meals to go eat whatever was left on the table. The poor, however, mainly ate bread and porridge, and met their protien needs by eating some kind of meat (usually pork) and Cheese.

Government Style

The Roman Republic was mainly a Aristocracy. Meaning, the Roman Republic was run by the rich and the wealthy. The word, translated from Greek, means, "Rule of the best-born". I.O.W, the upper class was the dominant people in the Roman Republic. If you were in the middle class or lower class, you were subject to being the upper class's slaves.
